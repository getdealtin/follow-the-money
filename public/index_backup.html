◆ About This Project

Built for everyone.
Funded by no one.

Follow the Money is a free, open-source civic transparency tool. We believe every person deserves to know how the brands they buy every day are influencing American politics — regardless of party, income, or background.

All data is sourced directly from the Federal Election Commission, a US government agency. No data is manipulated, filtered, or editorially selected. We show what the public record shows — nothing more, nothing less.

This tool is non-partisan. We display donation data for companies giving to Democrats, Republicans, and everyone in between equally. Our goal is informed citizens, not political outcomes.

Open Source

This project is fully open source. Developers can view the code, suggest improvements, or add more brands.
